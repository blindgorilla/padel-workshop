Coaching Approach

The Samuel Zaidman Method

What makes this different — and why it works for real padel players.

Athletic Transfer

Every drill is chosen because it improves how you actually move, react, and perform on court. Nothing is filler.

Mobility With Purpose

Mobility is trained specifically to improve your padel rotation, recovery position, and long-term joint health.

Explosive But Controlled

You'll build speed and power while staying balanced and protected from overload — the way elite athletes train.

Injury Prevention First

Shoulders, elbows, lower back, knees, hips, ankles — the common stress points in padel are specifically targeted.

Progressive Coaching

Sessions adapt to the group's level, making every exercise challenging, safe, and genuinely useful for everyone.
